The location

Port of Roses,
port of peace.

Portorož — "Port of Roses" — is Slovenia's crown jewel on the Adriatic. A town of warm stone, pine promenades, and a Mediterranean pace that invites you to slow down and breathe.

"The most beautiful stretch of coast you've never heard of — until now."

Our apartments sit just 200 metres from the beach, within easy reach of the old Venetian town of Piran, the salt pans of Sečovlje, and the finest seafood restaurants on the Slovenian coast.
